HVAC-R @PCTA


Do you like heat in the winter, AC in the summer?

In HVAC-R learn how to make every building comfortable, no matter what the weather is like outside. This funny acronym stands for

Heating Ventilation Air Conditioning and Refrigeration

Learn how to bend, cut, and form sheet metal, wire control circuits, thread iron pipe, and install heating and cooling systems. Explore this licensed trade that is critical to everybody's quality of life.

Something to think about...

Licensed Trade

  • HVAC/R Apprentice: $30,000+/year
  • HVAC/R Salesperson: $35,000+/year
  • Heating, AC, and Refrigeration Mechanics and Installers: $42,530+/year
  • 5yr Journey: $55,000+/year
  • Master HVAC/R Technician: $80,000+/year
  • HVAC/R Engineer: $115,000+/year

JROTC @Hope High School


Are you thinking of joining the US Army?

Students who choose to participate in Army JROTC will receive a four-year, sequential program of study that emphasizes:

  • academic preparation
  • citizenship
  • leadership
  • character development
  • service to the community
  • scope and nature of the Army

Students will develop personal responsibility and a sense of accomplishment, and the program will instill self-esteem, teamwork, and self-discipline.

Something to think about...

The average Active Duty is similar to working at a full-time civilian job. Service members receive an Army benefits and pay compensation package worth $99,000. Noncash compensation represents almost 60 percent of this package. Noncash compensation includes health care, retirement pay, child care and free or subsidized food, housing and education. Coupled with regular cash compensation, this adds up to attractive military compensation for Soldiers.
